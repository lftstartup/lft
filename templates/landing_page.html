ABOUT US

Our website offers you heritage in the language , you lack through teachers,institutes and authorities.
Moreover provides you with enojoyable language skills at any time and place . We offer a chance for teachers, programs and authorities keen on one of both languages to teach it and earn a profit in return. Any website user will be able to track their development and buy courses that are uploaded by the teachers. Moreover, teachers will have the choice to upload quizzes or games to assure that material is understood and enjoyed and eventually provide the students with feedback. With this idea, we wish to solve issues that are caused by miscommunication in this society and finally attain contact to accomplish communal targets.
